Safe Browsing Tips

How to protect yourself while browsing the internet.

1. Use Strong Passwords

Ensure your passwords are complex and unique. Avoid using easily guessable information like your name or birthdate.

2. Avoid Suspicious Links

Always verify a website's URL before clicking links. Phishing websites may look similar to trusted websites.

3. Use a VPN

Use a Virtual Private Network (VPN) when browsing public Wi-Fi to protect your personal information.
