Phishing Attacks

Learn how to identify phishing attempts.

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers try to trick people into giving away sensitive information, like passwords or credit card numbers, by pretending to be a trusted source.


How to Recognize Phishing Emails?

  • Look for suspicious sender email addresses.
  • Check for poor grammar and spelling errors.
  • Never click on unverified links.
